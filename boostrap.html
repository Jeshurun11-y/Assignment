Welcome to Bootstrap

Bootstrap is a super helpful front-end framework that makes building websites much faster and easier. It comes with ready-made layouts, color options, and tools that automatically adjust your site for different screen sizes. Basically, it saves you tons of time while making your site look neat and professional.

Text Colors in Bootstrap

Want to quickly change text colors? Bootstrap has you covered with simple helper classes like .text-primary, .text-success, and more.

<p class="text-primary">Primary Text</p>
<p class="text-success">Success Text</p>
<p class="text-danger">Danger Text</p>
<p class="text-warning">Warning Text</p>
<p class="text-info">Info Text</p>

Background Colors Made Simple

Just like text, you can easily change backgrounds with classes like .bg-primary, .bg-success, and so on.

<div class="bg-primary text-white">Primary Background</div>
<div class="bg-success text-white">Success Background</div>
<div class="bg-danger text-white">Danger Background</div>
<div class="bg-warning text-dark">Warning Background</div>
<div class="bg-info text-dark">Info Background</div>

Range Sliders in Bootstrap

Need a slider for users to pick a value? Bootstrap makes it easy with .form-range. It's neat and fully responsive.

<label for="customRange" class="form-label">Custom range</label>
<input type="range" class="form-range" id="customRange">

Using Bootstrap in Full-Stack Development

In full-stack projects, Bootstrap is your friend on the front-end. It keeps your user interface clean, mobile-friendly, and professional-looking. Meanwhile, your back-end (like Node.js, PHP, or Python) handles the logic and database stuff. The result? A smooth, responsive web app that looks great on any device.
